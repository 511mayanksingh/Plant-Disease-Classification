About Fasal Arogya

Fasal Arogya was developed as a cutting-edge farm support web application in January 2024 at IIT Kharagpur. Our mission is to empower farmers with advanced AI technology to protect their crops and ensure better yields.

With Fasal Arogya, farmers can quickly and accurately identify plant diseases by simply uploading a photo of a plant leaf. Our state-of-the-art machine learning model, powered by VGG16 architecture, analyzes the image and provides instant diagnosis along with recommended treatment actions.

We support detection of 15 different plant diseases across Pepper, Potato, and Tomato crops, helping farmers take timely action to protect their harvests and improve agricultural productivity.
